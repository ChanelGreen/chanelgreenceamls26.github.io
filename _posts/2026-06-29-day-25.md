      Our biggest challenge today was that our demonstration video would not play during our presentation. Instead of allowing it to disrupt our flow, we quickly           adjusted, explained what the demonstration would have shown, and continued presenting without losing momentum or taking extra time. It reminded me that               preparation is important, but being able to adapt under pressure is just as valuable.

I'm proud of how we supported one another, stayed composed when             things      didn't go as planned, and finished strong. Today's experience reinforced that research is about more than coding and analysis—it's also about             communicating your work clearly, thinking on your feet, and continuously learning from others.
