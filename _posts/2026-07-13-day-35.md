            One of the most valuable lessons today was understanding the importance of results flow. Initially, I organized the Results chapter by discussing each machine learning model separately. However, after reviewing a peer's report and receiving feedback, I restructured the chapter to present the experiment as a whole. This approach allows readers to first understand the purpose of the experiment, then interpret the collective findings before examining supporting visualizations.

Although much of today's work involved writing rather than coding, it significantly improved the readability and professionalism of the report. As I move into the SMOTE experiments, I feel more confident that the remainder of the Results chapter will follow a consistent structure that clearly connects each experiment to the overall research objectives.
