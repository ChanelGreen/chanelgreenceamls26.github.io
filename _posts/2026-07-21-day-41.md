what_i_learned: |
             Today reinforced an important lesson about research: developing a machine learning model is only one part of the process. Presenting results accurately               and consistently throughout a research paper requires just as much attention to detail. I learned how important it is to ensure that every table,                     figure, and written explanation reflects the most current experimental results. Small inconsistencies can impact the clarity and credibility of an                    entire study, making careful revision an essential part of the research process.
blockers:   | 
             Today's biggest challenge was reconstructing portions of our report to align with our finalized findings. Updating one section often required revisiting              multiple tables, figures, captions, and narrative descriptions to maintain consistency throughout the paper. While the revisions are strengthening the                quality of the report, they have been time-consuming and required careful attention to detail to ensure that every result is reported accurately.
reflection: |
            Although today felt more like editing than coding, I recognize that this stage is just as important as the technical work. Revising a research paper has              shown me that strong research is not only about obtaining good results but also about communicating those results clearly, accurately, and                            professionally. Even when the process feels repetitive, each revision moves the project closer to a polished final product. I'm looking forward to seeing             all of the pieces come together and finishing the report with confidence.
