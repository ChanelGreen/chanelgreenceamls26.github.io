what_i_learned: |
             Today reinforced that completing a research project is about much more than developing a strong model—it's about effectively communicating the work. As               we finalized our presentation, I learned the importance of balancing technical detail with clear, concise storytelling. I also gained a greater                       appreciation for how much preparation goes into presenting research, from refining visualizations and verifying results to ensuring every slide supports              the overall narrative of the project.
blockers:   | 
             One of our biggest challenges today was that we were completing several final pieces of the project at the last minute, leaving very little time to                   practice our presentation before the symposium. Thankfully, we were allotted additional time to rehearse as a team, which allowed us to become more                   comfortable with our speaking roles, improve our transitions, and build confidence before presenting.
reflection: |
            Today was all about bringing everything together. Looking back, it is rewarding to see how much our project evolved—from exploring the Environmental                  Justice Index dataset to developing and evaluating multiple machine learning models, interpreting the results, and preparing a polished research                      presentation. Wrapping up this project reminded me that collaboration, persistence, and attention to detail are just as important as technical skills.                I'm proud of what our team accomplished and grateful for the opportunity to contribute to research with meaningful public health applications.
